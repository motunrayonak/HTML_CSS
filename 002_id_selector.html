MY TOP 5 FAVORITE BOOKS

"To kill a mockingbird" by harper lee.

A powerful novel about justice and morality.

set in the american south during the 1930s.

"1984" by george orwell.

A dystopian story about government surveillance.

Explores themes of freedom and truth

"Pride & Prejudice" by Jane Austen.

A classic romance novel.

Deals with themes of social class and manners.

"The Hobbit" by J.R.R. Tolkien.

An adventurous fantasy story.

Introduces middle-earth and it's creatures.

"The great gatsby" by F.scott fitzgerald.

captures the jazz age & american dream.

Focuses on wealth, love, tragedy.
